# 🧠 MindCare Clinic - Psychiatry Simulator

## A Browser-Based Medical Simulation Game

**MindCare Clinic** is an immersive psychiatry clinic simulation game designed to teach players about psychiatric diagnosis, treatment planning, and clinical decision-making. Whether you're a medical student, aspiring therapist, or just curious about mental health, this game provides an engaging way to learn!

## 🚀 How to Play

### Starting the Game
1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Click "Begin Your Journey" on the loading screen
3. Complete the 5-slide tutorial
4. Start your first day of practice!

### Gameplay Loop

#### 1. **Patient Selection**
- Review the waiting room queue
- See patient names, chief complaints, and urgency levels
- Select a patient to begin consultation

#### 2. **Clinical Assessment**
Read carefully:
- Chief Complaint (patient's own words)
- Detailed History (timeline, symptoms, context)
- Mental Status Exam (appearance, behavior, mood, thought process)

#### 3. **Diagnosis**
- Choose from available psychiatric conditions
- Initially only Depression and Anxiety are unlocked
- Purchase DSM-5 Guide to unlock all conditions
- Consider differential diagnoses!

#### 4. **Treatment Planning**
- Select appropriate treatments:
  - Medications (SSRIs, mood stabilizers, antipsychotics)
  - Therapy (CBT, DBT, ERP, etc.)
  - Lifestyle modifications
- Consider effectiveness, side effects, and costs

#### 5. **Results & Learning**
- See if your diagnosis was correct
- Read detailed educational explanations
- Earn money and reputation
- Learn from mistakes with constructive feedback

#### 6. **End of Day**
- Review daily statistics
- Pay clinic expenses (rent, utilities)
- Calculate net profit/loss
- Start next day with new patients

## 🎯 Game Strategy Tips

### For Beginners
1. **Start simple**: Focus on Depression and Anxiety cases first
2. **Read thoroughly**: Key diagnostic clues are in the history
3. **Look for red flags**: Suicide risk, psychosis, substance use
4. **Consider duration**: Many diagnoses require specific timeframes
5. **Buy the DSM-5 Guide early**: Unlocks all conditions for $200

### Diagnostic Thinking
- **Depression vs. Bipolar**: Look for history of mania/hypomania
- **Anxiety vs. Panic**: Panic attacks are discrete episodes
- **OCD vs. GAD**: OCD has compulsions, not just worry
- **PTSD**: Requires trauma exposure + re-experiencing symptoms
- **BPD**: Pattern of instability across relationships, mood, identity

### Treatment Principles
- **First-line treatments**: SSRIs for depression/anxiety
- **Therapy matters**: CBT is effective for many conditions
- **Combination approach**: Meds + therapy often best
- **Safety first**: High-urgency patients need immediate attention
- **Cost considerations**: Some patients can't afford expensive treatments

### Economic Strategy
1. **Early game**: See more patients, build capital
2. **Mid game**: Invest in books and equipment
3. **Late game**: Upgrade clinic for higher fees
4. **Balance**: Don't sacrifice learning for money
